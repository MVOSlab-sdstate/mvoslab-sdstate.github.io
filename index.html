Welcome to MVOS Lab

Welcome to the Machine Vision and Optical Sensor (MVOS) Lab at South Dakota State University. We are dedicated to advancing precision agriculture through innovative research and cutting-edge technology that transforms how farmers monitor, manage, and optimize their crops. Our interdisciplinary team specializes in developing hyperspectral imaging systems, autonomous robotic platforms, and AI-powered analytics to detect crop diseases, assess plant health, and enable precision application of treatments. Located within the Department of Agricultural & Biosystems Engineering, we collaborate with students, faculty, and industry partners to create solutions that improve food security and enhance farm productivity.
